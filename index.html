Our Story

Celebrating Artistry Since 2015

Mohini Arts was born from a passion for connecting art lovers with exceptional handcrafted paintings and decor. Our journey began in a small Mumbai studio, where we curated works from local artisans who shared our vision of creating meaningful, lasting art.

Today, we represent over 50 independent artists and craftsmen across India, each bringing their unique perspective and mastery to our collections. We're committed to preserving traditional techniques while embracing contemporary expressions, offering our clients a diverse range of artworks and decor that inspire and transform spaces.

Every piece in our collection is carefully selected for its quality, originality, and emotional resonance. We believe beautiful decor should be accessible while honoring the skill and dedication of the artisans who create it.

— The Mohini Arts Team
